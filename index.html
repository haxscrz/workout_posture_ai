📋 How to Train Your AI

1
Record videos of yourself doing exercises. Record 2-3 short clips (~30 seconds each) for each form type.
2
Upload & label each video below. Mark whether it shows good form or a specific mistake.
3
Train the model — the AI will learn from your videos. You need at least 2 different labels with videos uploaded.
4
The model auto-deploys to the workout view. Next time you exercise, ML predictions will appear alongside rule-based feedback.
